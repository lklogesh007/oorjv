Every sample, checked for

What we test

The same four markers we explain to consumers — tested on your product before it earns the mark.

Adulteration

Cheap fillers, mixed-in oils, sugar syrups and other substances used to fake bulk or weight.

Toxic markers

Pesticide residues, banned colouring agents, preservatives and additives beyond safe levels.

Oxidised products

MDA levels that show whether an oil or fat has broken down into harmful compounds.

Healthy markers

Polyphenols and antioxidants that show your product still carries real nutritional value.
