About OORJV

It started with a simple question: what is actually in the food our own families eat every day?

How it began

Our story

OORJV began with a group of scientists who wanted a straight answer about the oil, milk and jaggery their own families were eating. Labels said "pure" and "natural" — but nobody was actually testing what was inside.

So they did. Using the equipment and expertise already available at AMMAGENOMICS, they began testing everyday food for adulteration, oxidation and nutritional value. What started as a personal project turned into OORJV: a certification mark built on lab results, not marketing copy.

"It started with a group of us wanting to test the oil, milk and jaggery our own families were eating. That's the whole reason OORJV exists." — From the OORJV team, on how it began

What we believe

FOOD is Medicine.

We believe food should nourish, not just fill. Every OORJV certificate exists to protect that idea — so a product's health value is something you can measure, not just something a package promises.

The people behind the mark

Who we are

Scientists from AMMAGENOMICS

OORJV certification is run by the same team behind AMMAGENOMICS Food Testing Lab, using established scientific methods.

Focused on traditional producers

We work closely with traditional farmers, home makers and small businesses who make food the honest way.

Independent of brands

A product only carries the OORJV mark once its actual batch has passed testing — never because of a brand relationship.
